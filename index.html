About Me

Hi! My name is Samuel Rodriguez, I am 23 years old, and I live in Lower East Side, Manhattan, where I was born and raised. In a rather non-traditional route, I graduated from Cornell University in 2018 with a Bachelors of Science in Biology, and after some time, and a slew of jobs I have decided to pursue technology instead of medicine. I currently work for an Educational Technology company named Amplify Education helping to develop their digital content for their Math Department.

My hobbies mainly revolve around keeping myself active (i.e. the gym and sports). I have played baseball my whole life, but now since school has finished, I have taken towards pick-up basketball considering it is the easiest to coordinate. Other than my athletic hobbies, I love to travel, seeing what the world and its cultures have to offer, and I love to create.

What does creating mean to me? Well, I am glad you asked. The entire reason I decided to pursue technology was to be able to create things (websites, applications, games, and other devices) that serve to better people's lives and make completing tasks easier. Aside from that, I took 4 years of art in highschool and I continue to utilize those tools that I learned to create art in any way that I can: graphic design for my Instagram page and posts, documenting my travels with photography and videos, and in a much less academic sense, DJing parties and events. I think creativity is something that can be exercised and is also something that a majority of people take for granted. In that aspect, I continue to exercise my creativity as much as I can because it will only help in what I wish to do. Creativity breeds good ideas, and good ideas breed more steps for mankind. I hope the skills that I learn here help in my overall goal to push the boundaries of technology forward.
